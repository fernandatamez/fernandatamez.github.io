Bora Bora...
It is a beautiful beach with many beautiful landscapes, a lot of fauna, animals that you would not find in many places and also with many activities to do.

Bora Bora enjoys a warm climate all year round, with average temperatures between 22°C and 30°C.

LIST OF THINGS TO DO IN BORA BORA
  • Visitar la ciudad principal, Vaitape.
  • Disfrutar de las vistas en Motu Tapu.
  • Explorar los Templos Marae.
  • Visitar el Centro de protección de tortugas.
ANIMALS THAT YOU CAN FOUND
  • Sharks
  • Jellyfish
  • Stonefish
  • Cone Snails
